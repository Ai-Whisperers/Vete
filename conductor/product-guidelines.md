# Product Guidelines

## Communication Style

To build trust and ensure clarity, the platform will adapt its communication style based on the user's role.

### For Pet Owners
The tone should be **Friendly & Accessible**. Communication will use a warm, approachable style with simplified language to make the platform feel supportive and easy to navigate.

### For Clinicians and Clinic Owners
The tone should be **Professional & Informative**. Communication will prioritize clarity, accuracy, and a more formal tone appropriate for a clinical and business context.
