# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
A multi-tenant SaaS veterinary clinic management platform designed to host multiple veterinary clinic websites from a single codebase using a JSON-based CMS pattern.

## Target Audience and Key Features

### Pet Owners
*   Schedule appointments
*   Purchase products
*   View their pet's records
*   Post lost and found pets

### Veterinarians and Clinic Staff
*   Manage all clinic-related operations (e.g., appointments, patient records, treatments)

### Clinic Administrators
*   Manage clinic operations, including billing, staff, and multi-location oversight
*   Configure clinic settings and permissions

### Clinic Super Users (Clinic Owners)
*   Access elevated privileges and configurations that employees should not be able to access.
