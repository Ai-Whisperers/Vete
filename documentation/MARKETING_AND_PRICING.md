# Marketing, Pricing & Revenue Strategy

**Date:** January 2026
**Based on:** 2026 Strategy Documents

---

## 1. Marketing Strategy

Our core strategy is **"Adoption First, Revenue Second"**. We lower the barrier to entry to zero to capture the market, then monetize through multiple streams.

### 1.1 Target Personas

We prioritize three key personas for our marketing messages:

| Persona                      | Who They Are                                 | Key Pain Point                       | The "Hook"                                                      |
| :--------------------------- | :------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Dra. María** (The Starter) | Recent grad, small consultorio, 0 employees. | Lack of professional image.          | "We built your professional website. It's yours, free forever." |
| **Dr. Jorge** (The Scaler)   | Established clinic, 10+ years, 3-5 staff.    | Operational chaos & leaking revenue. | "Stop losing money on expired stock and missed appointments."   |
| **Lic. Fernando** (The Exec) | Manager of a 5+ clinic chain.                | Data fragmentation across branches.  | "One dashboard to see every clinic's performance in real-time." |

### 1.2 Proactive Outreach (Growth Engine)

Instead of waiting for signups, we use a proactive "Pre-Generation" model:

1.  **Scrape**: Collect data on every existing veterinary clinic in the target country (starting with Paraguay).
2.  **Generate**: Automatically build a functional website (`vetic.com/clinic-name`) for them using public data.
3.  **Contact**: Reach out via WhatsApp/Email saying "Your website is ready. Claim it now."
4.  **Convert**: Offer them the keys to the platform.

### 1.3 Content Pillars

We distribute content across these four pillars to attract and retain clinics:

- **Educational (40%)**: Practical guides on clinic management (e.g., "How to price your surgeries").
- **Social Proof (25%)**: Case studies and metrics (e.g., "How Clinic X gained 47 new clients").
- **Product (20%)**: Short demos of features that solve specific pains.
- **Behind the Scenes (15%)**: Founder story, mission, and transparency to build trust.

### 1.4 Channels

- **WhatsApp**: Primary channel for Paraguay. Direct, conversational, high open rates.
- **Instagram/Facebook**: Visual educational content ("Carruseles") and paid retargeting.
- **Email**: Weekly newsletter with high-value management tips.

## 2. Pricing Strategy

We use a **Hybrid Freemium Model**. The Free Tier is NOT a "lite" version; it is the **Full Product** with ads. This ensures even free clinics are fully digitized, maximizing our datashare and network effects.

### 2.1 Pricing Tiers

| Tier            | Price (Monthly)       | Target      | Value Prop                                                  |
| :-------------- | :-------------------- | :---------- | :---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Gratis**      | **Gs 0**              | New/Small   | **Full Features** + Ads. Everything needed to run a clinic. |
| **Básico**      | **Gs 100,000** (~$14) | Solopreneur | No Ads + Priority Email Support.                            |
| **Crecimiento** | **Gs 200,000** (~$27) | Growing     | E-commerce Store + Bulk Ordering Access.                    |
| **Profesional** | **Gs 400,000** (~$55) | Established | WhatsApp Integration + Advanced modules (Hospital/Lab).     |
| **Empresarial** | **Custom**            | Chains      | Multi-location management + API Access + SLA.               |

### 2.2 The ROI Guarantee

To remove risk for paid tiers, we offer a guarantee:

> **"If you don't get X new clients in 6 months, the next 6 months are FREE."** > _Calculation: Monthly Fee ÷ Average Client Value (Gs 50k) = Required New Clients._

## 3. Revenue Streams (How We Make Money)

We do not rely solely on subscriptions. We have diverse revenue streams designed to monetize every type of user.

### stream 1: Clinic Subscriptions (Direct Revenue)

**Description**: Monthly fees from paid tiers (Básico, Crecimiento, Profesional).
**Why it works**: Established clinics will pay to remove ads and access premium business tools like E-commerce.
**Projection**: ~40% of clinics will eventually upgrade to a paid tier.

### Stream 2: E-commerce Commissions (Transactional)

**Description**: We take a commission on product sales made through the clinic's Vetic online store.
**Rate**: **3-5%** of GMV (Gross Merchandise Value).
**Why it works**: Clinics sell online without tech headaches; we earn from their success. Much lower than marketplaces (15-20%).

### Stream 3: Bulk Ordering / GPO (Margin)

**Description**: "Group Purchasing Organization" model. We aggregate orders from hundreds of small clinics to buy vaccines, food, and supplies in bulk from manufacturers at wholesale rates.
**Mechanism**: We act as the middleman. Clinics pre-order via Vetic.
**Margin**: **15-25%** markup on wholesale prices (still cheaper than what clinics pay individually).
**Potential**: This is expected to be a _massive_ revenue driver at scale.

### Stream 4: Advertising (Ad Revenue)

**Description**: Google AdSense (or direct pharma ads later) displayed on the **Free Tier**.
**Placement**: Dashboards, sidebars (NEVER inside a medical consultation screen).
**Why it works**: Monetizes the long tail of small clinics that never upgrade. Covers infrastructure costs.

### Stream 5: Data Licensing (Data Asset)

**Description**: Selling anonymized, aggregated industry insights.
**Data Included**: Breed distribution, disease outbreaks, vaccine coverage, treatment trends.
**Buyers**: Pharmaceutical companies, Pet Food brands, Insurers, Government agencies, Universities.
**Why it works**: Real-time epidemiological data is incredibly valuable and currently non-existent in developing markets.

### Stream 6: Government Bounties (Service Fees)

**Description**: Managing state-sponsored health programs.
**Projects**: Sterilization tracking, Vaccination campaigns, Disease monitoring.
**Mechanism**: We provide the platform to track stats; Gov pays a "bounty" per procedure recorded. We take a **10-15%** commission.

---

### Summary of Money Flow

1.  **Free clinics** generate **Ad Revenue** + **Data**.
2.  **Paid clinics** generate **Subscriptions** + **E-commerce Fees**.
3.  **All clinics** (Tier 3+) generate **Bulk Order Margins**.
4.  **The Aggregate Network** generates **Data Licensing** & **Gov Contracts**.

This diversified model creates a robust business that doesn't depend on squeezing small clinics for high subscription fees, aligning perfectly with our mission to democratize veterinary technology.
