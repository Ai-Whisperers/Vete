# Global Veterinary Services Master List

_A comprehensive catalog of veterinary services found in top clinics across LATAM, USA, and Europe._

## 1. General & Preventative Medicine (Essential)

- **Consultations**: General Check-up, Puppy/Kitten First Visit, Senior Pet Wellness.
- **Vaccinations**: Core (Rabies, Distemper/Parvo) & Non-Core (Lepto, Kennel Cough, Leishmania).
- **Parasite Control**: Internal (Deworming) & External (Fleas/Ticks/Heartworm).
- **Microchipping**: ISO Standard chips.
- **Health Certificates**: For international travel (SENACSA/USDA/DEFRA).
- **Nutrition**: Weight loss plans, Prescription diet counseling.

## 2. Advanced Diagnostics

- **Imaging**:
  - Digital Radiography (X-Ray).
  - Ultrasound (Abdominal, Cardiac/Echo).
  - CT Scan (Computed Tomography) - _High End_.
  - MRI (Magnetic Resonance) - _High End_.
  - Endoscopy (Gastrointestinal, Rhinoscopy).
- **Laboratory**:
  - Hematology & Biochemistry.
  - Urinalysis & Fecal Exams.
  - Cytology & Histopathology (Biopsies).
  - Rapid Infectious Tests (Parvo, Giardia, Leishmania).
  - Genetic DNA Testing (Breed/Health).

## 3. Specialized Medicine

- **Cardiology**: ECG, Doppler Echocardiogram, Holter Monitoring, Blood Pressure.
- **Dermatology**: Allergy Testing (Intradermal/Blood), Immunotherapy, Otoscopy.
- **Ophthalmology**: Tonometry (Glaucoma), Tear Production, Cataract Surgery.
- **Oncology**: Chemotherapy, Radiation Therapy (Linear Accelerator), Immunotherapy.
- **Neurology**: Seizure management, Spinal fluid analysis.
- **Reproduction (Theriogenology)**: Progesterone timing, Artificial Insemination, C-Sections.

## 4. Surgery & Anesthesia

- **Soft Tissue**: Spay/Neuter, Mass Removals, Wound Repair, Foreign Body Removal.
- **Orthopedics**: Fracture Repair, TPLO (ACL tear), Total Hip Replacement.
- **Dental Surgery**: Scale & Polish, Extractions, Root Canals, Orthodontics.
- **Anesthesia**: Inhalant (Iso/Sevo), Multiparameter Monitoring, Pain Management Blocks.

## 5. Emergency & Critical Care (24/7)

- **Triage**: Immediate assessment.
- **ICU (Intensive Care)**: Oxygen Cages, Fluid Pumps, Constant Rate Infusions (CRI).
- **Blood Transfusions**: Donor network.
- **Poison Control**: Toxicology management.

## 6. Holistic & Rehabilitation (Wellness)

- **Physiotherapy**: Hydrotherapy (Underwater Treadmill), Cold Laser Therapy.
- **Acupuncture & Chiropractic**: For pain/mobility.
- **Ozone Therapy**: _Popular in LATAM_.
- **Stem Cell Therapy**: Regenerative medicine.

## 7. Luxury & Convenience Services (Differentiation)

- **Telemedicine**: Video consultations for triage/follow-up.
- **Mobile Vet**: House calls (Van equipped for exams/lab).
- **Pet Taxi**: Transport to/from clinic.
- **Hotel/Boarding**: Luxury suites, "Day Care" play groups.
- **Grooming Spa**:
  - Ozone Baths.
  - Mud Masks.
  - "Fear-Free" Grooming.
- **Concierge**: Reminder management, Food delivery.
- **Euthanasia at Home**: Compassionate end-of-life care.
