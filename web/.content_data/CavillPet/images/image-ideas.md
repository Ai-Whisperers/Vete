## 1. Brand Identity Images

### 1.1 Primary Logo

**What it should show**: The Veterinaria Adris logo with the paw print and heart motif integrated into the typography. Clean, professional, and memorable.

**Style notes**:

- Deep green (#2F5233) as primary color
- Gold accent (#F0C808) for the paw/heart element
- Modern sans-serif typography (Montserrat style)
- Transparent background version needed
- Should work at sizes from 32px to 400px wide

**Emotional tone**: Professional, trustworthy, caring, modern

---

### 1.2 Logo Dark Variant

**What it should show**: Same logo optimized for dark backgrounds (footer, overlays).

**Style notes**:

- White or light gold text
- Paw/heart element in gold
- High contrast for readability

---

### 1.3 Favicon

**What it should show**: Simplified logo mark - just the paw print with heart, no text.

**Style notes**:

- Works at 16x16, 32x32, 48x48 pixels
- Recognizable at small sizes
- Green background with white/gold icon, or transparent

---

### 1.4 Social Media OG Image

**What it should show**: Banner for social media shares showing:

- Logo prominently displayed
- Tagline: "Cuidamos a tu familia de 4 patas"
- Professional clinic exterior or happy pet
- Contact info subtle at bottom

**Dimensions**: 1200x630px (Facebook/LinkedIn optimal)

## 2. Hero & Banner Images

### 2.1 Home Page Hero Background

**What it should show**:
A warm, inviting veterinary scene that communicates expertise and compassion. Options:

- **Option A**: A golden retriever or friendly dog being gently examined by a veterinarian in a modern, clean exam room
- **Option B**: Close-up of a veterinarian's hands gently holding a cat, with soft lighting
- **Option C**: Modern clinic exterior at golden hour with welcoming entrance

**Style notes**:

- Professional but warm color grading
- Slightly desaturated to allow text overlay readability
- Green/gold tones that complement the brand colors
- High resolution (at least 1920x1080)
- Subject on left/center, leaving right side for text overlay

**Emotional tone**: Trust, expertise, gentle care, modern facility

---

### 2.2 About Page Hero

**What it should show**:
The clinic's founding story visualized:

- **Option A**: Dr. Adriana Benítez with a rescued animal, showing the clinic's humble beginnings
- **Option B**: Timeline-style composition showing the evolution from small clinic to modern facility
- **Option C**: Team photo in front of the clinic entrance

**Style notes**:

- Documentary/authentic feel rather than stock photo style
- Natural lighting preferred
- Shows human-animal bond

**Emotional tone**: Origin story, passion, growth, dedication

---

### 2.3 Services Page Header

**What it should show**:
A professional veterinary services montage or single impactful image:

- Stethoscope on modern examination table
- Veterinary equipment arranged professionally
- Clean, modern treatment room with natural light

**Style notes**:

- Can be more clinical/professional than home hero
- Blues and greens that suggest cleanliness and technology
- Space at top for dark gradient overlay with text

**Emotional tone**: Professional, capable, well-equipped, trustworthy

---

### 2.4 Store Page Hero

**What it should show**:
Premium pet products arrangement:

- High-quality pet food bags (Royal Canin, Pro Plan style)
- Toys, accessories, and grooming products
- Clean, lifestyle-oriented product photography

**Style notes**:

- Bright, airy product photography style
- White/light backgrounds
- Shows variety and quality

**Emotional tone**: Quality, variety, convenience, premium care

---

### 2.5 Booking Page Background

**What it should show**:
Subtle, calming imagery that doesn't distract from the booking form:

- Soft focus veterinary office interior
- Abstract pet silhouettes
- Gradient with subtle pet-related patterns

**Style notes**:

- Very low contrast to serve as background
- Doesn't compete with form elements
- Calming colors (soft greens, whites)
